About Me

I'm an Audio Director with over 15 years of experience creating immersive audio for games, AR/VR, and interactive media. Most recently, I shipped Wildgate at Moonshot Games, where I built the audio department from the ground up.

From selecting middleware and hiring the team to directing 50+ hours of voice sessions and overseeing orchestral recording at Ocean Way Nashville, I bring a holistic approach to audio production.

My background spans AAA games, spatial audio for Magic Leap, co-dev work with Riot/Valorant, and academia. I specialize in technical implementation in Wwise/Unreal, creative direction, team leadership, and cross-discipline collaboration.
